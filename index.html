About

In the current situation of online education, students are facing a lot of difficulty to access notes. Especially,high school students and most importantly class 10th students.
We aim at helping them by providing notes to various subjects for free. 'My Notes SSLC' is a place where there is completely free and easy access to notes of any subjects. We do not aim for profit. There are lot of students facing issues to get any notes or study materials as there is not a simple website where they can get notes. Here there is no complexities.
